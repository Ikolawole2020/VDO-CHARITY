Our Mission

Caring for Innocent Children

VDO Charity Foundation is dedicated to caring for over 500 children suffering from Chronic Pelvic Pain Syndrome. We provide feeding, medication, shelter, and comprehensive well-being support.

Additionally, we support animal shelters to rescue and care for abandoned animals. Every donation directly impacts these innocent lives.
