Our Mission

Caring for Innocent Children

VDO Charity Foundation is dedicated to caring for over 500 children suffering from Chronic Pelvic Pain Syndrome. We provide feeding, medication, shelter, and comprehensive well-being support.

Additionally, we support animal shelters to rescue and care for abandoned animals. Every donation directly impacts these innocent lives.

Meet Our Founder

Vincent Philip D'Onofrio

Founder & CEO of VDO Charity Foundation. With a distinguished career in entertainment, Vincent channeled his passion for humanitarian causes into founding VDO in 2020 after a personal encounter with a child suffering from Chronic Pelvic Pain Syndrome (CPPS). Under his leadership, we've helped over 500 children and thousands of animals.

Vincent personally oversees shelter visits, medical partnerships, and fundraising events, ensuring every dollar makes a direct impact. His vision: \"No child or animal left behind in pain.\"

"Compassion isn't charity. It's justice. Every donation restores dignity to the voiceless." - Vincent D'Onofrio

Our Signature Events

July 2024

"Paws & Comfort" Therapy Day

Low-stress therapy dog sessions helped CPPS children manage chronic pelvic pain – petting/interactions lowered pain perception, reduced isolation. VDO organized accessible venue; 150+ kids + sponsors raised $75K for child meds/animal shelter.

Support More Days
May 2024

"Art, Paws, and Progress" Silent Auction

CPPS children created art as emotional outlet for invisible pelvic pain; animals added paw-prints. VDO gala auction raised $120K for child TENS machines/therapy + animal care. CEO Vincent hosted.

August 2024

"Walk, Roll, and Rescue" 1K/5K

VDO's mobility-friendly walk/roll for CPPS kids (wheelchair rests) paired with shelter dogs for socialization/awareness. 400 participants peer-fundraised $90K, boosted pelvic pain visibility, adoptions + child therapy funding.
